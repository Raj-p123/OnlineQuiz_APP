Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Online Quiz App?

It’s a web platform where users can take quizzes on different subjects and track their performance easily.

How do I create an account?

Click on the “Register” button in the navbar, fill in your details, and you’re ready to go!

Can teachers add their own quizzes?

Yes! Teachers can create and manage quizzes directly from their dashboard.

Is the app free to use?

Absolutely! All quizzes are free to attempt for students and learners.
